Debate Structure 3.7 (District Level)

Round 1: Opening Statement (15 Marks)

Total Time: 12 minutes

  • Host: 4 min — Topic, participants, OVNI definition.
  • Team A: 4 min — Opening opinion.
  • Team B: 4 min — Opening opinion.

Round 2: Clarification Round

Total Time: 12 minutes

  • Host: 2 min — Rules.
  • Team A asks Team B: 5 min.
  • Team B asks Team A: 5 min.

Round 3: Rebuttal Round

Total Time: 29 minutes

  • Host: 5 min — Overview of rounds.
  • Sub-Round 1: 8 min — Pair 1 vs Pair 1.
  • Sub-Round 2: 8 min — Pair 2 vs Pair 2.
  • Sub-Round 3: 8 min — Pair 3 vs Pair 3.

Round 4: Cross-Examination (25 Marks)

Break: 2 minutes

Strength of Questions: 12 marks

  • Host: 3 min — Rules.
  • Team A: 6 min — Up to 4 questions.
  • Team B: 6 min — Up to 4 questions.

Total Time: 15 minutes

Round 5: Closing Statements

Total Time: 8 minutes

  • Host: 2 minutes
  • Team A: 3 minutes
  • Team B: 3 minutes
